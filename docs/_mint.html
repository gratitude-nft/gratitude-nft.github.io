Frequently Asked Questions

What is an NFT?

An NFT, or Non-Fungible Token, transform digital works of art and other collectibles into one-of-a-kind, verifiable assets that are easy to trade on the blockchain. In other words, they're like collectible baseball cards that go up and down in value do to the rarity of the art, the access to utilities (benefits) that can be used in the real world (or the metaverse) and serve as "tickets" to events involving the community.

What does it mean to MINT?

To "mint" means your digital art becomes a part of the Ethereum blockchain - a public ledger that is unchangeable and tamper-proof.
